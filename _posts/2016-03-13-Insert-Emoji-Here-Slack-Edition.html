Insert Emoji Here - Slack Edition

Customizing Slack for your team can a beautiful exercise in ingenuity and creativity. You get to know a lot about your coworkers based off of which Slackbots were their creations. When you just can't find the proper way to express yourself with the default emojis, it's time to create your own.

Creating your own emojis for Slack is a relatively painless process and requires very little know-how technically. You just need a little wit and basic photo-editing skills.
